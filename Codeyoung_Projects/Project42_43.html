My school Avenue Primary Academyactually has pretty good teams: we have a football, netball, cricket, basketball and tons of other teams.

Football is a well-known sport all around the world. The game of football develops in the players a fair mind, team effort, and competitive spirit. It is regarded as one of the most disciplined sports in the world. Football is quite popular in all countries, and we have a boys and girls team.

Netball is a ball sport played by two teams of seven players. Games are played on a rectangular court with raised goal rings at each end. The object is to score goals from within a defined area, by throwing a ball into a ring attached to a 3.05 metres (10 feet) high post. We have a mixed boys and girls team.

Cricket is played by two teams of 11, with one side taking a turn to bat a ball and score runs, while the other team will bowl and field the ball to restrict the opposition from scoring. The main objective in cricket is to score as many runs as possible against the opponent. Before the match begins, the captain of both teams will toss a coin, with the winner of the toss being able to decide which team bats and fields first. We have a mixed boys and girls team.

Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court - it can be played indoors or outdoors. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal, an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket. We have a boys team and a girls team.
